Midnight Monsoon

Welcome to Midnight Monsoon.

This story takes place in the city of Vaireaux, a sprawling metropolis known for its endless neon lights, towering skyscrapers, and underground labyrinths. But beneath its beauty lies its greatest danger—the storms.

Vaireaux is infamous for its relentless, unforgiving tempests. When the rains come, the streets flood, power flickers, and the city transforms into a deadly maze. Those caught in the storm either find their way to safety… or become just another lost soul, washed away and forgotten.

You must navigate through the chaos, making life-or-death decisions at every turn. Many paths lead to ruin, but only a few will grant you escape. The storm does not show mercy, and neither does the city.

Throughout your journey, you will make crucial choices that determine your fate. Be warned—some decisions will lead to your untimely end, while others may bring you one step closer to survival. If you perish, you may restart your journey or load from your last saved checkpoint to try again.

Stay sharp. Trust your instincts. And most of all—beware the storm.

Best of luck to you… may you survive this.
